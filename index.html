About me

I am a qualitative researcher with over a decade of experience bridging the gap between complex human behaviour and organisational strategy. My work is powered by Behavioural Science, ensuring deep, rich insights translate into clear recommendations.

A personal and professional goal of mine it to support everyone - especially women and girls - to find their place in sport and build a healthy relationship with physical activity from a young age.
